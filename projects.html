My Projects

These are some of the key projects I've worked on throughout my journey.

Alzheimer's Detection using Deep Learning

This is my conference paper project where I used deep learning models such as VGG19, ResNet, DenseNet, and CNN to detect Alzheimer's from MRI scans.

Blood Connect Portal

A platform developed to find blood donors and blood banks, post blood requests, and enable willing donors to register and donate. It also tracks donors, blood banks, and blood donation camps based on specific dates.

Education Android Application

Developed an Android app named "Education" to assist intermediate students with learning resources and guidance.

E-Commerce Dashboard (Power BI)

Developed an interactive E-Commerce data visualization dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales performance across categories, markets, states, and shipping modes. Included visual KPIs (sales, profit, quantity, and shipping cost), donut and bar charts, tree maps, and slicers for dynamic filtering. Enabled enhanced data-driven insights to support decision-making.

Wearable Device for Women Protection

Participated in an ideathon at Vardan Company with a wearable device concept for women's safety. The idea used an extension ring mechanism, though the project was not selected in the final round.

Cybersecurity Internship

Completed an online cybersecurity internship course from Edustation. Gained foundational knowledge in protecting digital infrastructure and understanding cyber threats.

Social Media Website (Django)

Developed a full-stack social media web application using Django. Implemented user authentication, profile management, posts with images and captions, likes, comments, and a real-time feed of recent posts. Followed MVC architecture, used Django templates for the frontend, and integrated a relational database to store user and post data securely.
